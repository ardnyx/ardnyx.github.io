At PawDay, transparency and trust are paramount. In our commitment to providing exceptional pet products and services, we collect various types of information to ensure a seamless and personalized experience for our customers. The information gathered includes personal details, pet-specific information, transaction details, and non-personal website usage data.       • To tailor our products and services to the unique needs of your pets, we may collect information about your pets, including their names, breeds, ages, and any specific health considerations.
