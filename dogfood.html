Homemade Dog Food Recipes

Recipe 1: Beef & Veggie Crockpot Creation

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 1/2 cups brown rice
  • 1 (15-ounce) can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped butternut squash
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped carrots

Instructions:

  1. Stir in ground beef, brown rice, kidney beans, butternut squash, carrots, peas and 4 cups water into a 6-qt slow cooker.
  2. Cover and cook on low heat for 5-6 hours or high heat for 2-3 hours, stirring as needed.
  3. Remove from slow cooker, shred chicken and stir into rice and veggie mixture until evenly distributed.
  4. Let cool completely.
  5. Mix in some bone broth powder into the stew. It is so healthy and dogs love the taste.

Recipe 2: Peanut Butter Banana Bites

Ingredients:

  • Peanut butter
  • Bananas

Instructions:

  1. Mash bananas in a bowl.
  2. Add peanut butter and mix well.
  3. Spoon mixture into bite-sized portions.
